Sole Searching

2025-2026

Augmented Reality Application


Sole Searching in an Immersive Learning Iniative for NTU's Interdisciplinary Core Curriculum(ICC) Module CC0005, Healthy Living and Well-being. The project attemps to bridge the pedagogical gap between healthy living & wellbeing as knowledge and experience. By using embodiment to activate engagement and designing for social interaction first, the lesson encourages purposeful movement on campus and peer interaction across disciplines. It creates an easily implemented framework that scaffolds reflection deliberately and considers affective wellbeing as part of learning design.

During the implementation of the project, I led the visual branding of the application. I enhanced the pedagogical alignment of 3D AR objects to bridge theory and experiential learning, and improved technical features.
