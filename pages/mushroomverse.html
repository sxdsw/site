Miss Mushroomverse

2024

VFX Short Film

Beauty exists in everything. Mushrooms too, duh! The top three mushrooms battle it out as they strut and sashay for the coveted miss mushroom universe crown. Elves Hot Takes showrunners Pixie Shantel and Kathy Glamournova navigate us through the thrill and drama of the competition that keeps us hanging on the edge of our seats.
