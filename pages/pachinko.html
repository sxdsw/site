Pachinko

2024

MultiMedia Installation. Acrylic. LED Lights. Arduino. Java. Touch Designer.


Pachinko is an audiovisual installation that examines the complicity of the relationship between player and machine — a mutual agreement where the player surrenders to having their fate determined in exchange for the machine’s promise of escapism. Through this immersive experience, the work questions whether we truly have the autonomy to shape our outcomes, or whether we remain subject to the illusion of fate and the unseen forces that pull the strings.
